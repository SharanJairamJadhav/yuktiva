About Yuktiva

Who We Are

Yuktiva is an independent agricultural consulting and knowledge solutions company dedicated to empowering the agriculture sector through scientific excellence and strategic thinking.

Our multidisciplinary approach combines agricultural science, biotechnology, economics, market intelligence, and sustainability to help clients solve complex challenges and seize new opportunities.

We work with agribusinesses, startups, government agencies, research institutions, development organizations, and investors to design innovative, scalable, and commercially viable solutions.

Rather than focusing solely on operational activities, Yuktiva emphasizes strategic planning, technology assessment, economic analysis, and evidence-based decision support that enables long-term success.

Our Vision

To become a globally trusted knowledge partner delivering intelligent, sustainable, and commercially successful agricultural solutions.

Our Mission

  • Support innovation and commercialization through strategic advisory services.
  • Promote data-driven decision making across agricultural enterprises.
